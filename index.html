Welcome to the mini digital edition of Mary Shelley's Frankenstein. Made as a project for Text as Data II, taught at the University of Antwerp.

This website contains the following pages of Mary Shelley's 'Frankenstein' manuscript:

Each page appears with its corresponding transcription. These do not only display the text of the manuscript, but also the modifications made by Mary Shelley and Percy Shelley. Some general information about each page can be found at the top of the webpage. To view each manuscript page, press the 'pages' button in the navigation bar.

This digital edition is based on the corresponding manuscript pages and transcriptions from the Shelley Godwin Archive, provided by the Bodleian Library (among others).

The XML files for the transcriptions, encoded in TEI-XML, follow the guidelines of the BDMP Encoding Manual.
